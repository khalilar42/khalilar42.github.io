I just finished watching the movie "The Voice of Hind Rajab".

A 6 years old child was on call with the Palestinian Red Crescent Society for 3 hours asking to be rescued.

She was in a car surrounded by 6 dead family members corpses.

The Palestinian Red Crescent Society fought for hours to get the green light from israeli "humanitarian" to send an ambulance in place to save her.

Once they got the green light they sent the ambulance following the specific route given by the israeli "humanitarian" force.

At 200m of reaching Hind, an explosion was heard

Hind Rajab dead body was found roughly about 12 days later, along side 5 of her other family members, along side the paramedics at approximately 50m.
