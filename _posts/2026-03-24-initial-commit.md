This is my first blog. I named it "initial commit" — very original coming from a tech guy!

I finally decided to create a blog after many failed attempts… bUuuT Whhhy? ([don’t think about the penguin!](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mnTU_hJoByA))

I like to write. Not that I’m good at it, but I’ve always enjoyed putting my thoughts on paper. I also enjoy reading people’s blogs, though in this era of AI and short-form content my brain is getting fried and I can’t focus on anything for more than 3 minutes. So writing might help me reconnect some synapses again… doubt it will help, we’re all getting replaced anyway 🤖.

So, in this blog I’ll try to write about anything that crosses my mind. And just to be clear, everything I write here is personal and reflects only me, a lot has happened in my life lately, and I thought I might benefit from having a space to vent—even if nobody reads it except me, and who knows, maybe it’ll be helpful to someone.

An initial commit is supposed to be small and just set things up, right? See you in the next blog!
